Design Challenge

To begin the first step of our website redesign was something known as the design challenge. It is a template we used from the Human Centered Design book and assisted us to come up with our design question.


What is the problem you are trying to solve?

Healing unhealthy relationships or unhealthy mindsets of all ages.

Take a stab at framing it as a design question.

How can we reduce the idea of domestic violence?

Now, state the ultimate impact you’re trying to have.

Bringing attention to the importance of oneself mentally and physically.

What are some possible solutions to your problem?

Prevention programs, intervention plans, knowing the signs, loved ones, “put your money where your mouth is", etc.

Finally, write down some of the context and constraints that you’re facing.

Family, time, money, distance, technology, etc.

Does your original question need a tweak? Try it again.

How can we grow the inner strength of domestic violence victims/survivors?


Going through this challenge really help me wrap my head around which path I need to take for the redesign. It put me into the user's point of view which is crucial to understand.
