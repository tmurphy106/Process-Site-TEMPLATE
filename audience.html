Audience & Goals

After doing the research and reaching out to my clients, I was then able to separate the users into two groups and plan out the user’s top priorities when using the website.

Audience

There are 2 main objectives when it comes to this specific topic. Those are just those who need help and want to help.

This is the Need Help category. Now I had it break down to 3 basic categories of age. Children which range from newborn to 12 years-old, Teen which is 13-20 years-old, and Adults which is 21+. Underneath all of those sections, I labeled just a few of parties that may either be places they are affected, or types of people who hurt them. The pink one at the bottom represents themselves, because it can be built off of someone else, and they can move to doing it to themselves.

This is the Give Help category. It was first split into 2 categories, direct and indirect. People who can directly and indirectly help. Indirectly was focused on organizations, sponsors, etc. Organizations and sponsors can be along the lines of schools, programs, health businesses (orthodontics, hospitals, psychiatrist, etc.), donation stores, etc. Directly was focused on family, friends, volunteers, advisors, etc.

This is little section to talk about the people who are a part of the fight to help stop domestic violence. Th community helps with fundraisers, posters, merch, just being able to spread the word. People who have maybe previously been through a similar scenario. Also, maybe someone who has seen or been near someone going through it. They all do their part in standing strong for multiple people, not even just locally, even around the world.

When it comes to skeptics, its more about the person that is a part of the action, doing the abuse to someone else. The person or multiple people could have mental issues, which trigger a number of things. They could not have a good personal life, for example lonely, so they don’t know how to nicely interact with others. People that surround you on a daily could really do anything, you never know, you just have to always be cautious unless something is confirmed.


Goals

Overarching Goals

Client

  • Bring awareness of who they are and what their mission is
  • Receive donated items whether it be money or objects to give away that are in good condition
  • Give out sources to those in need
  • Get other big organizations to help support their topic
  • Connect with other organizations that have similar missions and donate and support them

Audience

  • Get help ASAP
  • Want to get in touch for some not major help
  • Looking for resources for someone else
  • Donating
  • Wanting to become a sponsor
  • Wanting a presentation

Website

  • Make information easy and quick to find and understand
  • Raise money and resources through donations
  • Make it simple to request resources
  • Connect with other organizations

Specific Goals

Users Goals Content/Action
Survivor/Supporter (Majority Female) Get advice or assistance (whether it be for them or someone else); immediate or loosely Persuade them to join the program and become involved with someone to help them; and maybe even continue to stay with them after their problem is solved
The Community/Family/Friends Spread awareness; attend to someone they know is in need; donations; find more volunteers; recommendations; show them what they can do to help Being a very big encouragement for the patients; making bigger donations/events
Volunteers/Sponsors/Organizations Spreading their mission around and visually showing them off; showing them what they can do to help Become a sponsor or staff member/volunteer to help the association grow and spread
